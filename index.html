About Us

Mission Statement

Rooted in our passion for food and shared moments is our mission is to bring people together by crafting high-quality wings that are not just a meal but an experience. We want to foster togetherness through the bold symphony of flavored wings that ignite the senses and create lasting memories. Definitely not your ordinary chicken!

Our Story

Hammad, a sales executive from Sri Lanka, harbored a deep love for chicken wings, yet found himself in a disheartening predicamentâ€”there was no dedicated restaurant that truly offered an enjoyable wing experience. The wings everywhere were prepared in a monotonous fashion, lacking diversity in flavors, leaving him longing for a more satisfying culinary experience.

In collaboration with Diane, an entrepreneur and close friend from the USA who became a part-owner, we embarked on a six-month project to explore various sauce recipes and flavors to discover the perfect complements for our chicken dishes. Diane, an ardent admirer of my mother's cooking, often hailed it as the best in all of Sri Lanka and even the world.

Impressed, she suggested that my mother, affectionately known as Chooty, should open her own restaurant, confident it would be an instant hit. Armed with a clearer vision of the flavors we wanted to pursue, we enlisted my mom's expertise, and she perfected our marinade and sauce recipes. The result was a selection of distinct sauces that breathed new life into the conventional world of wing flavors, including two unique Sri Lankan additions: Kochi and Devilled.

Following the successful introduction of chicken wings, we expanded our menu to include two sizes of complete meals served with fried rice or chapati, along with crispy chicken wings, chicken popcorn, and devilled chicken sausages. Our offerings also feature side orders of french fries, fried rice, chapati, and two beverages: Coke and Sprite. The response was overwhelming, marking us as a tremendous success.

Although we initially operated as a home-based business, the soaring demand for our products at competitive prices led us to open a small take-away outlet in December 2019, broadening our accessibility beyond our already thriving UberEats platform.

Our aspirations reach beyond borders; we envision establishing multiple outlets worldwide, aiming for global recognition as a Sri Lankan-born takeaway chain. Quickly evolving into an overnight sensation, we are confident that you, too, will relish every delectable bite.
